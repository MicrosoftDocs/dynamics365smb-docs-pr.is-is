2. <span data-ttu-id="153ec-109">Veldu ![Ljósaperuna sem opnar eiginleika Viðmótsleitar](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Segðu mér hvað þú vilt gera") táknið, sláðu inn **Grunnstillingarvinnublað** og veldu síðan tengda tengilinn.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="153ec-109">Choose the ![Lightbulb that opens the Tell Me feature](media/ui-search/search_small.png "Tell me what you want to do") icon, enter **Configuration Worksheet**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>  
3. <span data-ttu-id="153ec-110">Valið er **Afrita gögn úr fyrirtæki**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="153ec-110">Choose the **Copy Data from Company**.</span></span>
