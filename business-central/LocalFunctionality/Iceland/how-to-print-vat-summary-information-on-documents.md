# <a name="print-vat-summary-information-on-documents"></a><span data-ttu-id="3420c-103">Prenta VSK-yfirlit á skjölum</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-103">Print VAT Summary Information on Documents</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="3420c-104">Ef virðisaukaskattur er reiknaður eru upplýsingar um samantekt VSK-upplýsinga yfirleitt prentaðar á sölu- og innkaupaskjöl.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-104">If VAT is calculated, VAT summary information is typically printed on sales and purchase documents.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3420c-105">Á Íslandi prentar [!INCLUDE[d365fin](../../includes/d365fin_md.md)] hins vegar ekki samantekt VSK-upplýsinga ef aðeins er notaður einn VSK-sölukóði í skjalinu.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-105">However, in Iceland, [!INCLUDE[d365fin](../../includes/d365fin_md.md)] does not print VAT summary information if only one VAT sales code is used in the document.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="3420c-106">Hægt er að breyta þessari hegðun með því að nota valkostinn **Sýna alltaf VSK-yfirlit**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-106">You can modify this behavior by using the **Always Show VAT Summary** option.</span></span>  

<span data-ttu-id="3420c-107">Eftirfarandi ferli lýsir því hvernig á að birta samantekt VSK-upplýsinga á söluskjali, en sömu skref eiga einnig við um staðfestingar á sölupöntunum, sölutilboðum, sölukreditreikningum, standandi sölupöntunum, innkaupapöntunum, innkaupareikningum og innkaupakreditreikningum.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-107">The following procedure describes how to display VAT summary information on a sales invoice document, but the same steps also apply to sales order confirmations, sales quotes, sales credit memos, blanket sales orders, purchase orders, purchase invoices, and purchase credit memos.</span></span>  

## <a name="to-show-vat-summary-information"></a><span data-ttu-id="3420c-108">Til að sýna samantekt VSK-upplýsinga</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-108">To show VAT summary information</span></span>  

1.  <span data-ttu-id="3420c-109">Velja skal ![Leit að síðu eða skýrslu](../../media/ui-search/search_small.png "Leit að síðu eða skýrslu táknið") tákn, slá inn **Bókaðir sölureikningar** og velja svo viðeigandi tengil.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-109">Choose the ![Search for Page or Report](../../media/ui-search/search_small.png "Search for Page or Report icon") icon, enter **Posted Sales Invoices**, and then choose the related link.</span></span>  
2.  <span data-ttu-id="3420c-110">Á listanum skal velja viðkomandi skjöl og svo aðgerðina **Prenta**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-110">In the list, select the relevant document, and then choose the **Print** action.</span></span>  
3.  <span data-ttu-id="3420c-111">Til að birta VSK-upplýsingar í skýrslunni skaltu velja gátreitinn **Sýna alltaf VSK-yfirlit**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="3420c-111">To display VAT information in the report, select the **Always Show VAT Summary** check box.</span></span>
