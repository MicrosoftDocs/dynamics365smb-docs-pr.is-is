1.  <span data-ttu-id="16298-115">Í sölupöntunarlínu fyrir birgðir sem stillt eru til að vera safnað í birgðir, færið inn magn í reitinn **Magn** sem er umfram birgðir.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="16298-115">On a sales order line for an item that is set up to be assembled to stock, enter a quantity in the **Quantity** field that exceeds inventory.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="16298-116">Síðan **Kanna ráðstöfunargetu** birtist.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="16298-116">The **Check Availability** page appears.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="16298-117">Frekari upplýsingar, sjá [Skoða tiltækileika vöru](inventory-how-availability-overview.md).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="16298-117">For more information, see [View the Availability of Items](inventory-how-availability-overview.md).</span></span>
